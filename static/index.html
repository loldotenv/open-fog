open-fog

Backfill your Fog of World map.

Pull a flight or rail track and export it as a Fog of World–compatible KML.

Reads the Flightradar24 history page — free tier, last ~7 days. Enter the UTC date: for late-night East Asia departures, pick the day FR24 sees, not your local one.

        Station suggestions via Photon (OSM). Pick one for each, then route — the Railway lines overlay is on by default so you can check coverage.
